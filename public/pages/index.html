Get involved today! See down below for ways to connect with us.

OrgSync

To become an official member of ACM at ECU you must register through OrgSync. You must log in using your ECU Banner ID and password or OrgSync account in order to see the document required in the next step. Once you're there you will find a form called "ACM Membership Application" that you must fill out, and then wait for it to be approved to officially become a member.

Learn more

Discord

Discord is a free app that is available through the app store on your phone, as an in-browser application, and even as a free download for Windows/Mac.

Join our Discord today to ask questions about the club, or to even meet new people in the CSCI community! Here you can talk in a casual environment about various topics such as CSCI classes, the latest technology, or even meme if you're into that.

Common Questions

Do I need to be a Computer Science major to join? - Not at all, anybody can join!

Is the club free to join? - Yes! It's free to be an ordinary member

Can I share events that I believe ACM will enjoy taking part in, or request to be a speaker for an event I'd like to host for the club? - Sure! If you would like to propose an event, please contact our chair Tiffany Nguyen.
